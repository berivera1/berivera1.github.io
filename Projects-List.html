Tic-Tac-Toe

A simple Tic-Tac-Toe game made for school using BlueJ. The instructions are pretty self-explanatory (I mean, its Tic-Tac-Toe), but if you have difficulty wrapping your head around this apparently mind-bogglingly complex game, I will explain it to you. One player plays as a person named "Mr X". Another player plays as someone named "Mr O". There is a 3x3 panel in which Mr X places Xs and Mr O places Os. When there are "three in a row", the winner is crowned victor and the looser is prepared to be sacrificed to the lord of hell, Satan. Fun fact, hell is also the realm which invented this terribly devilish game in the first place, as a reminder for the people of the world to satisfy the devil by completing the annual sacrifice and insuring that he doesn't come up and just brutally murder everybody (he's kinda a buzz kill like that).

A Bit of Luck: Last of the Species Edition

Based off of the Scratch game A Bit of Luck, this version includes more levels, more detailed backgrounds, more detailed textures, two entire new in-game worlds, an entire final boss fight, and a new soundtrack (over 5 times the size of the original)! After the nuclear apocalypse annihilated the entirety of the human race, as well as all species on earth, a fish, mutated to be able to live on land, awakes in the middle of a solitude forest, and must learn to survive in the apocalyptic wasteland void of life, but not danger. Wandering around a forest, a factory, and a cave, you explore vast wastelands to answer the question, is there any life still out there, or are you all alone in the end?

Lunar Lander

As with Race Car Game!, this game was also made using the terribly crappy art of a 7th grader, odd controls, and a hidden impossible level which I have not even beaten myself (but I know is beatable). This is a simple lunar lander game, with 2 levels, as well as a hidden third level. You play as a rocket ship and must navigate to a landing strip. Warning: When you beat the first level, the second level begins automatically. Also, if I remember correctly, you only have 1 life, and you have to restart if you die (which will most likely happen a lot).
